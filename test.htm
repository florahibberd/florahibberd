EPK: New single release 11.6.21 Flora Hibberd – Night, Perpetual Night, Perpetual is the new single from Flora Hibberd’s latest album Hold. It will be released on 11 June 2021 on all digital platforms. Hold will be released on 25 June 2021. Hold was recorded DIY-style over the new year period in a country house in Sens, Burgundy, and mixed in a small apartment in Paris. It deals with intimacy and distance, love and longing, jubilation and desire. Bio Flora Hibberd was born in London in 1995, and has been living in Paris since she was eighteen. Her debut EP The Absentee (Clearlight Records) was released in 2019 to critical acclaim. In September 2020 she released her second EP, Archipelago. One Chord To Another named Archipelago EP of the Year 2020. Her next record, the seven-song Hold, is to be released in June 2021.
