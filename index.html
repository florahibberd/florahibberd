Bio


Flora Hibberd was born in London, and lives in Paris. In 2019 she released her debut EP, 'The Absentee,' through London-based indie Clearlight Records. Critics described being 'swept away by her deeply moving emotional presence,' (Folk Radio UK) and called Flora 'a marvellous newcomer to the realm of indie folk.' (PopMatters) In september 2020 she started working with Paris-based producer Victor Claass, putting out a second EP, 'Archipelago,' followed in 2021 by the short album 'Hold.' Her debut album 'Swirl' is out since January 17th, 2025 on 22Twenty.
